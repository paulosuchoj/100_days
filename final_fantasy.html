Final Fantasy I

When the Four Warriors Of Light began a legend, they knew little of what awaited them beyond the walls of Cornelia. The Crystals' chosen ones, they were entangled in a scheme of time and space and a recurring battle, and the power of their hearts and brave souls is the last bastion preventing the world's demise.

Final Fantasy II

The Emperor's greed has surpassed the material realm, and he now aims to conquer both Heaven and Hell, crushing all who stand in his way. Will the power of the Rose be enough to unite the rebels against the threat of Mateus of Palamecia?

Final Fantasy III

Four orphaned children, touched by the Crystal's light, must set forth to bring balance back to a world where a shadow looms over, with an evil deity trying to push everything into a maelstrom of chaos.

Final Fantasy IV

Trapped between light and dark, Cecil must face kingdom, friends and even himself to prevent an alien force of destroying everything he loves. When he starts seeing corruption all around and unknowingly setting in motion the events of a terrible tragedy, he takes it upon himself to fix what's wrong.

Final Fantasy V

One day, the wind died down. The plains and mountains became silence, and people all around the world felt something was amiss. When a milenar menace tries to break from its shackles and terrorize a world divided in two, the warriors must collect twelve legendary weapons and throw darkness back to where it belongs.

Final Fantasy VI

Magic has all but died in the world, and few remnants can still use the arcane powers. Despite this situation, the Empire has developed Magitek technology, allowing soldiers to employ powerful artificial magic. But what happens when a homicidal clown seizes control over the army, and has hold over the last person with magical blood still alive?

Final Fantasy VII

When a famous warrior, missing in action for years, returns from the shadows to wreak havoc and seek vengeance, Cloud and his mates ensue a chase, the blonde SOLDIER seeking answers, while attempting to stop the megacorp Shin-Ra from sucking the Planet dry of the precious Lifestream it subsists on. Will they be able to reach the truth about the mysterious disappearances occurring all around and the destruction of an ancient civilization?

Final Fantasy IX

The queen of Alexandria is slowly going mad, and even her own daughter is afraid of what the future the holds. When a troupe of actors (who also happen to be thieves) sets out to kidnap Princess Garnet on her 16th birthday, they could not fathom the princess herself wished to be captured. When the queen uses everything in reach to get a hold of Garnet, Zidane and his companions start to see the hidden strings attached to the queen's motives.

Final Fantasy X

Only a Summoner, wielding the holy boon of the Final Aeon, can stop Sin's relentless destruction of Spira for a time and unravel the Calm, but this cycle repeats endlessly. One day, Sin reaches and attacks Zanarkand, where Tidus lives, taking him to Spira by unknown means. There he meets the timid Summoner Yuna, who must shortly depart on a pilgrimage. He joins the young woamn and her Guardians, in hopes of finding a way back home along the road.

Final Fantasy XII

In a matter of years, the Archadian Empire took hold of all lands in the west, the blooming desert kingdom of Dalmasca amongst them. In a seemingly endless tragedy, after war and betrayal as the kingdom was handed over the Empire in a silver platter, an orphan named Vaan is caught up in political intrigue while attempting to steal from the royal palace, and he is inexorably taken in the tides of destiny that would engulf land and people.

Final Fantasy XIII

The government of Cocoon is purging every citizen who came in contact with an ancient relic called the Pulse Vestige where a deity of Pulse, The World Below, lies dormant. But being sent to death is not a part of Lightning's plans. Together with the other citizens being purged, she seizes and crashes the train, freeing herself and others in the process. Now, branded as enemies of humankind, l'Cie, her group is hunted by Sanctum forces.

Final Fantasy XV

War has ravaged the land between the kingdoms of Lucis and Niflheim over possession of the last Crystal in existence. When a truce is finally signed and peace rises on the horizon, Niflheim stabs Lucis in the dark, and launches a massive attack on the kingdom's capital of Insomnia, overpowering their forces and taking the Crystal. Urged to leave the city, prince Noctis and his friends now travel, seeking to retrieve what was stolen.
